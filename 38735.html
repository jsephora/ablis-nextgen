Privacy (Tax File Number) Rule 2015 - Australian Government

Description

You must comply with this rule if you intend to collect individual's tax file number (TFN) information. This rule regulates the collection, storage, use, disclosure, security and disposal of individual's TFN information with the goal of ensuring the privacy of the TFN information. Please consult the Contact Officer for more information and to ascertain the level of compliance that may be required.
